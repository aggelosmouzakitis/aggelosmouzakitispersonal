Burnout diagnostic

A diagnostic for high-functioning people whose drive may be getting expensive.

This assessment explores the less obvious patterns underneath burnout: self-worth through achievement, shame and pressure, interpersonal spillover, emotional flattening, nervous-system strain, and constant activation.

It takes around 8 minutes. At the end, you’ll get a burnout score, a grade, and a breakdown by section.
